About our Restaurant

Made with Love

The Restaurant

Meenachil Restaurant is based in St Micheal, Montreal. It is a Kerala Style South Indian Restaurant that provides a wide variety of choices in its menu. The menu is divided into appetizers, Soup, Tandoori items, different types of Curry, different types of rice such as jeera rice, plain rice, ghee rice, veg pulav rice and coconut rice. There is also a wide varieties of dosas available such as plain dosa, masala dosa, garlic kara dosa, ghee dosa, onion dosa, cheese dosa, ghee masala dosa, paneer masala dosa, chana masala dosa and chilli-onion Egg dosa. Different types of biriyani include Beef biriyani, Egg Biriyani, Chicken Biriyani and Vegetable Biriyani. Finally there are the desserts such as Gulab Jamun, Rasmalai and Mango Kulfi.

There is a huge selection of breads and drinks available such as Coke, Pepsi, Ginger Ale, Masala Chai, Sweet Lassi and Lait Rose Milk.

Our History

  • Our Experience

  • The Founders Met

    The founders met back in Kottayam, which is more than 10,000KM away in a small state called kerala in India.

  • Create A Restaurant

    They came up with the idea to create a restaurant in montreal that represents the true taste of south indian cuisine.

  • Added 50+ Employees

    It was the year 2005, when the restaurant really started making profit. After a few rough years, the restaurant was finally beginning to grow and getting reviews.


  • More Restaurants Outlet

  • Stablished Restaurant in India

    Began a restaurant back in kerala, that was based on the success and experience from running the restaurant in montreal.

  • Franchise Restaurants Ettumanoor

    A major international branch was opened in Montreal, after success of local restaurant business.

  • Added 100+ More Employees

    More employees were added to grow the restaurant branches in 2 more locations in India.

  • Stablished Marketing

    Menu was changed and new items added to provide customers outside india, more choices so that they could choose from a wide array of options.
